3ï¸âƒ£ Impacts â€‹

Guaranteeing bereaved family participation rights strengthens trust in accident investigations. This not only benefits the bereaved families but also increases public trust in the investigation results, reducing unnecessary societal controversy. Moreover, incorporating family opinions during the determination of accident causes can reduce further legal disputes and lead to more effective recovery and prevention measures.

Conversely, if participation rights are not guaranteed, families may harbor distrust toward the investigation process, potentially escalating controversies over accountability for the accident. Critics argue that while bereaved family participation rights enhance trust and fairness in accident investigations, emotional involvement could hinder objectivity or delay the process. Excessive accommodation of specific family demands could also lead to conflicts with other stakeholders.

A proper balance is necessary to maintain the fairness, objectivity, and efficiency of the investigation process.
